SciVis 2025

Workshop on Advances in Scientific Visualization Techniques and Applications (SciVis Workshop)

In conjunction with IEEE Pacific Visualization Conference 2025




Workshop Date

Tuesday, April 22, 2025

Workshop Theme

This workshop aims to foster interdisciplinary dialogue and collaboration between scientific visualization researchers/practitioners and domain experts from applied fields.

We invite submissions from visualization specialists to present cutting-edge advances in techniques, algorithms, and systems.

Simultaneously, we encourage participation from domain scientists in computational science, biomedicine, climate science, engineering, and related disciplines to articulate their visualization challenges, domain-specific application scenarios, and unmet needs.

By establishing this cross-disciplinary platform, the workshop seeks to identify emerging opportunities for visualization research, inspire domain-driven technical solutions, and strengthen collaborations between visualization specialists and end-user communities.
